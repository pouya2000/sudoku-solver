This free online Sudoku solver can solve any valid Sudoku puzzles with a smart option to show the rationale of each step - a good way to learn the solving techniques by examples.

Quick Start

Select a digit on the side of the grid first. Then click on the cells where you want to place the selected digit. After entering your puzzle, press either the "Solution" button for an instant solution or the "Solve with Smart Solver" button to bring up our smart solver.
